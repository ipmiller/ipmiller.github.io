About Ian Miller

Ian Miller is a music director, pianist, arranger and orchestrator based in New York City. A versatile musician, Ian draws upon a classical piano training and an extensive background in musical theater, as well as a love of gospel, jazz, and R&B.

Ian’s recent music direction credits include The Caucasian Chalk Circle (Juilliard Drama Division), Felix Starro (world premiere, Ma-Yi Theater Co), and Little Shop of Horrors (Virginia Theater Festival). Ian provided the orchestrations for East West Player’s 2023 world premiere of On This Side of the World in Los Angeles, with a cast recording soon to be released.

As a pianist, Ian performed onstage in the recent Broadway revival of Into the Woods, and he has accompanied rehearsals for the Broadway productions of Wicked, The Music Man, and West Side Story. Since 2022, he has been on faculty at the Juilliard School, as a vocal coach and accompanist in the Dance Division.

Since 2017, Ian has been the music director of AHRC’s “Our Broadway”, a musical theater program for adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities in New York City. Ian is passionate about bringing people together through through song, and he is eager to continue building relationships with emerging composers, theatermakers, vocalists, and community organizations.
