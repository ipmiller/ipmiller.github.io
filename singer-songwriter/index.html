About Ian

Ian Miller is a St. Louis-born, New York-based singer-songwriter whose writing reaches across genres, drawing from folk rock, funk, gospel, soul, and the storytelling-through-song of musical theater. At times bold and energizing, at times intimate and contemplative, Ian’s music speaks to longing and liminal space - “the now and the not yet” of being alive.

His first EP, In Between, was released in the spring of 2020, and his full-length album, Threshold, came out in spring 2024. Ian was selected to participate in the 2020 Johnny Mercer Songwriters Project and received a residency in Croatia from the Lopud Foundation in 2023.
